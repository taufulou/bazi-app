## 1. Peach Blossom Star (桃花星) Types and Classification

### 1.1 Core Peach Blossom: 咸池桃花 (Xianchi / Bathing Pool)

The foundational peach blossom star. Also called 敗神 (Defeat Spirit) or 桃花煞. It represents the 沐浴 (Bathing) stage in the Twelve Life Stages (十二長生).

**Lookup Rule** (from year branch OR day branch, check other branches):

| Year/Day Branch Group | Peach Blossom Branch |
|----------------------|---------------------|
| 申子辰 (Monkey, Rat, Dragon) | 酉 (Rooster) |
| 寅午戌 (Tiger, Horse, Dog) | 卯 (Rabbit) |
| 巳酉丑 (Snake, Rooster, Ox) | 午 (Horse) |
| 亥卯未 (Pig, Rabbit, Goat) | 子 (Rat) |

**Note**: Peach blossom branches are always 子午卯酉 (the four cardinal branches / 四正). The lookup uses the Three Harmony (三合) groups.

### 1.2 牆內桃花 (Inside Wall Peach Blossom) — MAINSTREAM

**Definition**: When the peach blossom star appears in the **year branch** or **month branch** (the "inner" pillars representing family/home).

**Significance**: Favorable romance. Represents love within the bounds of marriage. The person is romantic but channels it within committed relationships. Good for marriage quality.

**Classification**: 正桃花 (Auspicious peach blossom)

### 1.3 牆外桃花 (Outside Wall Peach Blossom) — MAINSTREAM

**Definition**: When the peach blossom star appears in the **day branch** or **time branch** (the "outer" pillars).

**Significance**: Problematic romance. Represents extramarital affairs, casual attitudes toward relationships, third-party involvement. Unfavorable for marital happiness.

**Note**: Some sources reverse this — placing 月支/日支 as "inside the wall" (within the marriage/relationship scope) and 年支/時支 as "outside the wall". The most common Taiwan/HK interpretation uses year+month = inside, day+time = outside.

**Classification**: 爛桃花 (Problematic peach blossom)

### 1.4 天喜桃花 (Heavenly Joy Peach Blossom) — MAINSTREAM

**Definition**: A highly auspicious romance star. Derived from the year branch using a reverse-count pattern. Always paired with 紅鸞星 (Red Phoenix) as opposites.

**Lookup Table** (by natal year branch):

| Year Branch | 紅鸞 Star | 天喜 Star |
|-------------|----------|----------|
| 子 (Rat) | 卯 | 酉 |
| 丑 (Ox) | 寅 | 申 |
| 寅 (Tiger) | 丑 | 未 |
| 卯 (Rabbit) | 子 | 午 |
| 辰 (Dragon) | 亥 | 巳 |
| 巳 (Snake) | 戌 | 辰 |
| 午 (Horse) | 酉 | 卯 |
| 未 (Goat) | 申 | 寅 |
| 申 (Monkey) | 未 | 丑 |
| 酉 (Rooster) | 午 | 子 |
| 戌 (Dog) | 巳 | 亥 |
| 亥 (Pig) | 辰 | 戌 |

**Pattern**: 紅鸞 = reverse-count from year branch (子→卯, 丑→寅, 寅→丑...). 天喜 = 紅鸞's 六沖 (opposite branch).

**Properties**:
- 紅鸞 is Yin Water (癸水), governs marriage
- 天喜 is Yang Water (壬水), governs engagement, celebration, and childbirth
- Both are considered auspicious (吉星)
- In youth (~20s), encountering them suggests marriage; in middle age (post-marriage), suggests childbirth

**Classification**: 正桃花 (Auspicious)

### 1.5 沐浴桃花 (Bathing Peach Blossom) — MAINSTREAM

**Definition**: Based on the Twelve Life Stages (十二長生). When a day stem's "bathing position" appears in the chart's branches.

**Day Stem → Bathing Position**:

| Day Stem | Bathing Branch |
|----------|---------------|
| 甲 (Yang Wood) | 子 |
| 乙 (Yin Wood) | 巳 |
| 丙 (Yang Fire) | 卯 |
| 丁 (Yin Fire) | 申 |
| 戊 (Yang Earth) | 卯 |
| 己 (Yin Earth) | 酉 |
| 庚 (Yang Metal) | 午 |
| 辛 (Yin Metal) | 亥 |
| 壬 (Yang Water) | 酉 |
| 癸 (Yin Water) | 寅 |

**Significance**: Represents openness to sensuality and sexual matters. If not well-controlled, can lead to emotional distress from uncontrolled desires. Connected to beauty and artistic talent.

**In different pillars**:
- Year pillar: Parents may have marital discord
- Month pillar: Career changes before middle age, life has waves
- Day pillar: Hard early years, peaceful later years
- Time pillar: Weaker fortune in old age, thinner bond with children

**Classification**: Can be either 正 or 爛 depending on ten god relationship

### 1.6 九丑桃花 (Nine Ugliness Peach Blossom) — MAINSTREAM

**Definition**: A specific set of day pillars (干支 combinations). Despite the name "ugly," people with this actually tend to be attractive but prone to romantic scandals.

**The Nine Pillars**:
丁酉、戊子、戊午、己卯、己酉、辛卯、辛酉、壬子、壬午

**Lookup**: Check if the day pillar (日柱) matches any of the nine combinations above.

**Significance**: The name "九丑" doesn't mean ugly appearance. Paradoxically, these day pillars often indicate attractive people. However, they are prone to:
- Romantic scandals (爛桃花)
- Emotional entanglements
- For women: potential childbirth complications (古法)
- For men: physical defects or incomplete endings (古法)

**Classical source**: 古書 notes "女人犯之主產危，男犯相丑陋不全終"

**Classification**: 爛桃花 (Problematic)

### 1.7 秋葉桃花 (Autumn Leaf Peach Blossom) — NICHE / APP-SPECIFIC

**Research finding**: This term does not appear in mainstream classical Bazi texts. It may be:
1. A proprietary term coined by a specific app/master
2. A metaphorical name for peach blossoms that fade quickly (like autumn leaves falling)
3. Possibly referring to peach blossom in 衰/病/死/墓 stages of the twelve life stages

**Likely interpretation**: A peach blossom star that appears in a weakened state — the person has romantic encounters but they don't last. Could be calculated as a peach blossom that sits on a 衰 or 死 position relative to its own element.

**Implementation note**: This may need to be reverse-engineered from the rival app's output rather than from classical sources.

## 2. 正桃花 vs 爛桃花 Classification

### 2.1 Core Methodology

The classification depends on the TEN GOD relationship between the peach blossom branch and the day master:

**正桃花 (Auspicious Romance) indicators**:
1. Peach blossom is the 正財 (for males) or 正官 (for females) = spouse star
2. Peach blossom is the 正印 = gentle, nurturing romance
3. Peach blossom is 喜用神 = beneficial element
4. Peach blossom is in 年支/月支 = 牆內桃花
5. 紅鸞 or 天喜 = inherently auspicious
6. Peach blossom is not 沖/克/合化 by other branches = stable

**爛桃花 (Problematic Romance) indicators**:
1. Peach blossom is 劫財 = 桃花劫, financial loss through romance
2. Peach blossom is 七殺 (for females) = 桃花煞, dangerous romance
3. Peach blossom is 傷官 (for females) = rebellious, damages marriage
4. Peach blossom is 忌神 = unfavorable element
5. Peach blossom is in 日支/時支 = 牆外桃花
6. Multiple peach blossoms in chart = excessive romantic energy
7. Peach blossom is 沖/刑 = volatile romance
8. 紅艷煞 without positive ten god = purely sensual attraction

### 2.2 Combined Assessment

The classical approach combines positional analysis (which pillar), ten god analysis (what relationship), and element analysis (喜用 or 忌):

```
If peach_blossom.ten_god in [正財, 正官, 正印] AND peach_blossom.is_favorable:
    → 正桃花 (good romance leading to marriage)
If peach_blossom.ten_god in [劫財, 七殺, 傷官] AND peach_blossom.is_unfavorable:
    → 爛桃花 (problematic romance leading to loss)
```

## 3. 婚姻宮 (Marriage Palace / Spouse Palace)

### 3.1 Definition

The **日支** (day branch) = Marriage Palace (婚姻宮), also called 配偶宮 (Spouse Palace) or 夫妻宮. It represents the "home" or "position" of the spouse.

### 3.2 Day Branch → Spouse Appearance

| 四正 (Cardinal) | 子、午、卯、酉 | Attractive, good-looking, stylish |

| 四墓 (Storage) | 辰、戌、丑、未 | Plain/sturdy, honest, may be less attractive |

### 3.3 Day Branch → Spouse Personality (by category)

**子午卯酉 (Cardinal branches)**:
- Spouse values aesthetics and romance
- Appreciates life's pleasures, romantic disposition
- Tends to be shorter in stature
- Lifestyle emphasizes mood and atmosphere

- Spouse is practical and shrewd
- May travel frequently for work
- Average appearance but intelligent
- Medium build
- Pragmatic approach to life

- Spouse is honest, dependable, stubborn
- May be plain-looking but solidly built
- Fixed temperament, doesn't understand romance easily
- Taller/larger build

## 4. 配偶星 (Spouse Star)

### 4.1 Core Rules

| Gender | Spouse Star | Backup |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Male (男命) | 正財 (Direct Wealth) | 偏財 if no 正財 |
| Female (女命) | 正官 (Direct Officer) | 七殺 if no 正官 |

### 4.2 Spouse Star Analysis

**Strong Spouse Star** (得令、有氣、逢生、健旺):
- Capable spouse with good health
- Strong presence in relationship
- Supportive partner

**Weak Spouse Star** (不得月令、無氣、遇克、逢沖):
- Spouse may have health issues or weak capabilities
- Relationship may lack support
- May need to work harder in marriage

**No Spouse Star visible** (命中不見):
- Classical interpretation: deep karmic reasons, may indicate:
  - Spouse often away (long-distance)
  - Late marriage or difficulty finding partner
  - Other life focuses dominate over marriage
- Use the backup star (偏財/七殺) for analysis

**Multiple Spouse Stars** (配偶星多現):
- For males (多正財 or 財星混雜): multiple romantic relationships, difficulty choosing
- For females (官殺混雜): complex love life, difficulty finding satisfactory partner
  - Classical: "正官偏官混雜者，異性緣佳；感情較複雜不專"
  - Multiple suitors, but marriage often troubled
  - Post-marriage vulnerability to temptation

### 4.3 Spouse Star Position

| Position | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Year Pillar | Early marriage or meeting spouse young; spouse from distant place or family connection |
| Month Pillar | Meeting spouse through work/social circles in prime years; moderate timing |
| Day Branch (配偶宮) | Best position — spouse star sits in spouse palace |
| Time Pillar | Late marriage; spouse related to children or later life |

### 4.4 Spouse Star as 喜用神 vs 忌神

- **Spouse star = 喜用神**: Spouse brings prosperity, good fortune to the native. Marriage enhances life.
- **Spouse star = 忌神**: Spouse may bring burdens, financial strain, or conflict. Marriage requires more effort.

子未、丑午、寅巳、卯辰、申亥、酉戌

### 5.2 喜用神-Based Compatibility

The most sophisticated matching method. Based on the principle that a good spouse's chart should supply the elements you need:

1. **Determine each person's 喜用神** (favorable elements)
2. **Check if Partner A's strong elements = Partner B's 喜用神** (and vice versa)
3. **Three quality levels**:
   - **Best**: Both partners' 旺神 (strong elements) are each other's 用神 — inseparable couple
   - **Good**: Both share the same 喜用神 with no 刑沖破害 — common ground and language
   - **Acceptable**: Five element flow between both charts is smooth

**Birth Season Recommendations**:
- If 喜用神 = Fire/Wood → seek spouse born in spring/summer (wood/fire seasons)
- If 喜用神 = Metal/Water → seek spouse born in autumn/winter (metal/water seasons)
- If 喜用神 = Earth → seek spouse born in seasonal transitions (辰戌丑未 months)

### 5.3 合盤 (Chart Comparison) Methodology

**Layer 1 — Day Pillar Interaction**:
- **天合地合** (stems combine + branches combine): Best — deep emotional connection
  - Example: 甲子 day meets 己丑 day (甲己合 + 子丑合)
- **天干相生** (stems produce each other): Good — supportive dynamic
- **地支六合** (branches form six harmony): Good — natural compatibility
- **Avoid 日支相沖** (day branch clash): Creates fundamental conflict

**Layer 2 — Year Branch Interaction**:
- Check for 三合, 六合, 六沖, 相刑, 相害
- 三合/六合 = inherently compatible
- 六沖 = friction but not necessarily fatal (can be resolved by other elements)

**Layer 3 — Five Element Balance**:
- Combined chart should achieve better five element balance than either individual chart
- One person's excess should fill the other's deficiency

**Layer 4 — Spouse Star Cross-Check**:
- Male's 正財 element should align with female's day master element (and vice versa for 正官)
- Strongest match: his spouse star = her day master, her spouse star = his day master

## 6. 桃花運年份 (Romance Luck Years)

### 6.1 紅鸞桃花年 (Red Phoenix Year)

**Calculation**: Use the natal year branch → lookup 紅鸞 star from Table 1.4 above. When the current year's branch matches the 紅鸞 star, it's a 紅鸞年.

**Effect**: Primary marriage indicator star. In youth (~18-28), strongly suggests marriage or engagement. In middle age, may indicate childbirth or family celebration. Strongest of the romance year indicators.

### 6.2 天喜桃花年 (Heavenly Joy Year)

**Calculation**: 天喜 = 紅鸞's opposite (六沖). When current year's branch matches 天喜, it's a 天喜年.

**Effect**: Secondary marriage indicator. Brings joyful romantic encounters, reduces obstacles in relationships. Less intense than 紅鸞 but still very positive. May also indicate pregnancy/childbirth.

### 6.3 正緣桃花年 (True Destiny Romance Year)

**Calculation**: When the **spouse star** (正財 for males, 正官 for females) appears in the annual pillar's stems or branches. Specifically:
- The annual stem IS the spouse star
- The annual branch's hidden stems contain the spouse star
- The annual stem/branch combines with (合) the day master or day branch

**Effect**: The year when a "fated" partner is most likely to appear. Distinguished from generic peach blossom years because it specifically activates the spouse star, not just romantic energy.

### 6.4 咸池桃花年 (Generic Peach Blossom Year)

**Calculation**: Use natal year/day branch → lookup peach blossom from Table 1.1. When the current year's branch = the peach blossom branch.

**Effect**: Increased romantic encounters and opposite-sex attraction. However, this only indicates MEETING people — it does not indicate marriage destiny. The native has the power of choice during their own peach blossom year. Less specific than 正緣年.

### 6.5 Strength Hierarchy

From strongest to weakest marriage/romance indicators:
1. Natal chart peach blossom (命中桃花) — strongest, lifelong
2. Major luck period (大運) peach blossom — decade-level influence
3. Annual transit (流年) peach blossom — year-level influence

## 7. 桃花劫年份 (Romance Danger Years)

### 7.1 紅艷桃花年 (Red Beauty Danger Year)

**Calculation**: When the annual branch matches the native's 紅艷煞 position (from Table 1.8), AND the native is already in a vulnerable chart configuration (e.g., weak day master, spouse palace under stress).

**Effect**: Increased susceptibility to destructive romantic temptations. Extra-marital affairs, romantic scandals, financial loss through romance.

### 7.2 桃花劫年 (Peach Blossom Robbery Year)

**Calculation**: When the peach blossom branch in the annual pillar has a 劫財 (Rob Wealth) or 七殺 (Seven Killings) relationship to the day master, AND it is an 忌神 (unfavorable element).

**Effect**:
- 劫財 桃花: Financial loss through romantic entanglements, being deceived
- 七殺 桃花: Dangerous romance, potential for legal trouble, domestic violence, or serious scandal

**Classical formula**: "命局桃花星為劫財或偏官，又是忌神者，行桃花運時，要么因女性大破財，要么遇到騙子被騙財騙色"

### 7.3 婚姻宮逢刑剋年 (Marriage Palace Under Attack Year)

**Calculation**: When the annual branch forms 刑 (punishment) or 剋 (overcome) with the day branch (marriage palace).

**Specific interactions**:
- 六沖 (Clash): 子午沖、丑未沖、寅申沖、卯酉沖、辰戌沖、巳亥沖
- 三刑 (Triple punishment): 寅巳申刑、丑戌未刑、子卯刑
- 自刑 (Self-punishment): 辰辰、午午、酉酉、亥亥

**Effect**: Marriage instability, spousal conflict, potential separation. When combined with other negative indicators, may indicate divorce.

## 8. 感情生變年份 (Relationship Change Years)

### 8.1 婚姻宮逢合年 (Marriage Palace Combining Year)

**Calculation**: When the annual branch forms 六合 or 三合 with the day branch.

**六合 combinations with day branch**:

| Day Branch | Annual Branch for 合 |
|-----------|---------------------|
| 子 | 丑 year |
| 丑 | 子 year |
| 寅 | 亥 year |
| 卯 | 戌 year |
| 辰 | 酉 year |
| 巳 | 申 year |
| 午 | 未 year |
| 未 | 午 year |
| 申 | 巳 year |
| 酉 | 辰 year |
| 戌 | 卯 year |
| 亥 | 寅 year |

**Effect for UNMARRIED**: Possible cohabitation, engagement, or marriage — the 合 "draws" a partner in.

**Effect for MARRIED**: A "movement" in the marriage palace. Can indicate:
- Positive: Deeper bonding, cooperative energy, smooth communication
- Negative: If the combining element is 忌神, may indicate someone else "combining with" (attracting) the spouse — potential affair

**Key distinction**: 合 is fundamentally "movement through attraction" — softer than 沖 but still represents change.

### 8.2 婚姻宮逢沖年 (Marriage Palace Clash Year)

**Calculation**: When the annual branch forms 六沖 with the day branch.

| Day Branch | Annual Branch for 沖 |
|-----------|---------------------|
| 子 | 午 year |
| 丑 | 未 year |
| 寅 | 申 year |
| 卯 | 酉 year |
| 辰 | 戌 year |
| 巳 | 亥 year |
| 午 | 子 year |
| 未 | 丑 year |
| 申 | 寅 year |
| 酉 | 卯 year |
| 戌 | 辰 year |
| 亥 | 巳 year |

**Effect for UNMARRIED**: Paradoxically, can trigger marriage — "衝開" (clash open) the marriage palace, especially if the natal chart already has 日支逢合 (day branch in harmony with another branch). The clash breaks open the stored energy.

**Effect for MARRIED**: Instability, arguments, restlessness, potential separation. The spouse "wants to leave" or the native becomes dissatisfied. Not necessarily divorce unless combined with other negative factors.

**Classical rule**: "婚姻宮喜靜忌沖" — the marriage palace prefers stillness, dislikes being disturbed.

### 8.3 原局 vs 流年 Severity

| Scenario | Severity | Outcome |
|----------|----------|---------|
| Natal chart day branch already clashed (原局逢沖無救) | Most severe | High likelihood of divorce/remarriage |
| Natal chart fine, but 大運+流年 clash day branch | Moderate | Temporary instability, recoverable |
| Only 流年 clashes day branch | Mildest | Brief period of tension |

### 8.4 Marriage Timing Rule (合沖 Application Period)

Classical rule for determining WHEN someone marries:
1. If natal chart has 日支逢合 (合住): marriage occurs in the year when 流年 CLASHES open the combination (衝開之年)
2. If natal chart has 日支逢沖: marriage occurs in the year when 流年 COMBINES with the day branch (合住之年), neutralizing the clash
3. In marriage-age years, when the spouse star (正財/正官) appears in the annual stem/branch and combines with the day master or day branch → marriage year

## 10. 本命姻緣分析 (Natal Marriage Destiny Analysis)

### 10.1 Male Chart Rules (男命)

**財星透出 (Wealth Star Visible in Stems)**:
- 正財 clearly visible → Will marry a good wife
- Day master strong + 正財 visible + 正財 as 喜用神 + no clash/combination disrupting it → Wife is beautiful and virtuous, harmonious marriage, career success after marriage
- 正財 visible but day master weak → May not be able to "handle" a capable wife; wife may dominate

**財星不透出 (Wealth Star Hidden)**:
- Spouse star only in hidden stems → Spouse is reserved, relationship develops slowly
- No 正財 at all → Use 偏財 as backup; may indicate informal relationships or late marriage

**財星多現 (Multiple Wealth Stars)**:
- Multiple 正財 or mixed 正財/偏財 → Multiple romantic interests, difficulty with fidelity
- Classical: tendency toward extramarital relationships

**時支藏財 (Wealth Hidden in Time Branch)**:
- Spouse star appears only in time pillar → Late marriage (often 30+)
- If spouse star is strong in time pillar → Marriage is eventual but delayed
- Meeting the right person later in life

### 10.2 Female Chart Rules (女命)

**官星透出 (Officer Star Visible in Stems)**:
- 正官 clearly visible + day master balanced → Will marry a good husband
- 正官 as 喜用神 → Husband is capable and supportive
- 正官 visible but weak/clashed → Husband present but may be unreliable

**官星不透出 (Officer Star Hidden)**:
- Hidden 正官 → Husband is low-profile, relationship is private
- No 正官 at all → Use 七殺 as backup; may indicate dominant/aggressive spouse

**官殺混雜 (Officer and Killings Mixed)**:
- Both 正官 and 七殺 present → Complex love life, many suitors before marriage
- Post-marriage vulnerability to temptation and affairs
- Classical: "多情慾，易生三角戀愛"

**時支藏官 (Officer Hidden in Time Branch)**:
- Same as males — late marriage indicator
- May find husband through children-related circumstances

### 10.3 Early vs Late Marriage Indicators

**Early Marriage (早婚) Indicators**:
1. Spouse star appears in year/month pillar, undamaged
2. Four pillars contain 三合 or 六合
3. Day master is strong AND spouse stars are abundant
4. Spouse star is in 生旺 (prosperous) position
5. Day branch (marriage palace) = 喜用神

**Late Marriage (晚婚) Indicators**:
1. Spouse star appears only in time pillar
2. Spouse star is damaged by 刑/沖/克
3. 比劫 (competition) is abundant in 大運
4. Day branch is 忌神 or contains 劫財
5. Spouse star is in 休/囚/死/墓/絕 position
6. 傷官 dominant in female chart (剋夫)
7. 比劫 dominant in male chart (剋妻)

### 10.4 Marriage Quality Indicators

**Good Marriage**:
- Spouse star = 喜用神 + sits in spouse palace (日支)
- Day branch in 六合 or produces day master
- No 沖/刑 on marriage palace
- Single clear spouse star (not mixed)

**Troubled Marriage**:
- Marriage palace (日支) clashed in natal chart with no rescue
- Spouse star = 忌神
- 官殺混雜 (female) or 財星混雜 (male)
- 日支 contains 劫財 or 傷官
- Multiple clashes involving the marriage palace
